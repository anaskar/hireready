What is a 1099?

A 1099 is an IRS form that's mailed to your home by the company who you contracted with. For example, if you drove with Uber, they are required to mail you, and the IRS, a document reporting your earnings.

There are a few instances where they won't send you douments but you're still required to submit a form (see below)
