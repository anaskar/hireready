Preparing for your Homejoy Orientation

Congratulations! You passed our training session. Your background check is being processed, and you are scheduled for an orientation session with Homejoy. Please read on so you can be totally prepared. It's nothing new! We've already covered all of this at your training. Homejoy now wants to see if you can remember it all. Definitely practice those 5 star tips, and, as always, let us know if you have any questions. Good luck!
Location

Your orientation session will be located at 580 California St, 7th floor, San Francisco, at the corner of California and Kearny. Please be 15 minutes early and meet in the lobby so we can regroup and go upstairs together. Homejoy will not allow for any late comers so it's important to leave your home early! Street parking is difficult, but it's just a few short blocks away from the Montgomery St Muni/Bart station, and numerous buses. See the map on the botton of the page for more details.



Direct Deposit

In order for you to get paid, you must have direct deposit (checking or savings account). If you do not, we recommend going into any store such as Safeway, CVS, and Walgreens. There are many out there but we recommend Serve by American Express. (Click Here to learn more). It's a few dollars to purchase, it comes with free cash withdrawals at thousands of ATMs across the country, and there is no monthly fee as long as you are depositing at least $500/month in the account. If you would like us to purchase one for you, please bring $25 cash to the orientation session and let us know ahead of time that you want one. You'll get $20 of that back, already loaded on the card.
