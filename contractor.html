What is an independent contractor?

An independent contractor is someone who provides a service, as an individual, to someone else. Since the creation of companies like Uber and Task Rabbit, this distinction has become confusing.

The U.S. Department of Labor outlines very specific characteristics of a job that determines which one you are.

If you work with Uber, Lyft, Sidecar (or any other ridesharing sevice), Homejoy, Handy, Taskrabbit (or any other home services platform), or Postmates, Wun Wun (or any other delivery service), you are most likely an independent contractor.
