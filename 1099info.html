3 Steps to becoming an Independent Contractor

For more detailed information, check out our friends at Zen99

1. Register your Business Name

Skip this step and Use your own name

If you want to operate your business under a name other than your personal name, you will have to register your business.

2. Complete the Tax Certificate

Many cities and states require this to operate as an independent contractor.

You must apply and submit a payment to register as a business in order to file taxes with the IRS .

3. Complete your taxes quarterly

Completing taxes should be quick and easy.

Each quarter complete the forms 1040-ES and file Schedule C, Profit or Loss from Business and Schedule SE, Self Employed Tax from the IRS.
